# Roberto Duran

## Summary
Alex uses a brief clip of Roberto Duran to highlight clean boxing mechanics. He notes that Duran follows the four-phase sequence (load, explode, accelerate, follow-through) with a right cross that demonstrates proper hip-driven stretch and contraction, as well as the arc trajectory Alex frequently emphasizes.

## What Alex Teaches
Alex analyzes a short example of Duran’s mechanics to illustrate integrated punching. He points out that Duran drops his center of gravity to load, then pushes off to explode, letting his hip move forward while his arm comes back — creating stretch between the arm and torso. This stretch is then contracted upon to accelerate the punch. Alex specifically notes that the right cross continues its trajectory in an arc after impact, which is a key element he teaches for maintaining tension and follow-through through the target. The clip serves as a clear template of the four-phase model: load, explode, accelerate, and follow-through.
